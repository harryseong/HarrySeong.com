welcome
The typewriter displayed is the Remington Rand Deluxe Model 5, a model manufactured in the 1940's. I was inspired to acquire this typewriter after reading about Tom Hanks' quirky fascination and passion for these mechanical word factories.
My relationship with technology is one which continues to exist with a faint whisper of the hackneyed, hyphenated adjective, "love-hate." You are welcome to enter and peer about into the world of the man who supposedly sits on the proverbial fence regarding his relationship to the aforementioned modern necessity.
